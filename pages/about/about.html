WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

A little history of why I love doing what I am doing. My name is Jan Gorsek and I am from Slovenia. I started with skateboarding when I was a young kid. Everyone was doing it at that time. I bought my first skateboard when I was 13 years old. And I learned Olie (stationary jump) in like 4 hours. And it was an awesome feeling. And because skateboarding was very tough my friends abandoned it and I also didn’t want to do it alone. So I quit skateboarding. At the age of 16, I learned to snowboard and I never went back to skiing.

After that, I went to a kitesurfing school and I loved it. I still love it today. The speed the wind the jumping the freedom it gives you. It’s just the best feeling ever. But I had one problem. There was almost no wind where I was on vacation. in Croatia in summer the wind is very poor. I had to wait 2 weeks for a medium-strong wind to show up on Windguru (A forecast site for weather). And even then I needed to be lucky for the wind to show up. So like 50% of the time I got stuck on the beach waiting for the wind. That’s why I started looking on the internet for alternative ways to ride when there is no wind. That’s how I found the electric skateboard and foil. And I have to say its the best feeling ever. Not a lot of people have the guts to try it. But once you do you will get hooked. So I want the world to be able to ride whenever they feel like it and not wait for the optimum conditions. It doesn’t matter if you live in the City in a Village or in the Hills. You should be able to have a good time whenever possible. That is my goal and I am hoping to achieve it for everyone.

Our Eskates and eScooters are very affordable and are maintance free. They are very easy to operate. My 2 kids learned to ride the eSkate in 2hours.
