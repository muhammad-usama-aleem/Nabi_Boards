HOW TO BUY?

As much as the Internet has made everything accessible to us, we still doubt things are happening there, and online shopping is one of them. Buying gadgets online is relatively an alien concept for our people. Perhaps because of scams, at NabiBoards, we fully understand our customers’ concerns, hence trying to make the shopping experience as enjoyable as possible. We also try to keep on improving our customer support for our valuable customers.

Three Simple Ways to Purchase from NabiBoards.

Visit Our Stores in Slovenia:

Once you have reviewed the products online and liked or selected a few from the products listed on our website, make sure you call the NabiBoards store in your respective city. Why call them before your visit? To make sure that the product is available for you at the store. If it is not available, our team will tell you when the product will be available and when you can come to purchase it.

NabiBoards Slovenia Address: Sentjanz pri Dravogradu 1 2373 Sentjanz Slovenia

Place Your Orders Directly on NabiBoards Website:

Placing your order through the website of NabiBoards is a more comfortable and hassle-free process. All you need to do is simply select the product you want to purchase, select on Add to Cart, click on Proceed to Checkout, add in your Name, Address and Contact details, select your Payment Method and your order has been placed.

Select product > Add to Cart > Proceed to Checkout > Add Shipping Details > Payment Options > Place Order

Place your order via Call or WhatsApp:

You can also place your orders via call or WhatsApp. To order via call, you can call us at ‘00386 41 321 169’ and tell us about your requirements or just the product details; our representative will take your contact and shipping details from you. You will also be briefed about the payment procedure, and your order will be confirmed. Other than that, If you want to place an order via WhatsApp, simply click on WhatsApp us Now, and you will be directed to the WhatsApp number of NabiBoards with the product link copied and pasted in the chat.
